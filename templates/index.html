About us

Welcome to Hibiscus Royal Academy

Hibiscus Royal Academy is a co-educational boarding school located in Abuja, Nigeria. It was established in 2008 and is known for its serene environment, conducive for learning. The school offers a blend of Nigerian and British curricula, providing a well-rounded education to its students.
