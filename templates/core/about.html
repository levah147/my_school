About us

Welcome to Hibiscus Royal Academy

Hibiscus Royal Academy is a co-educational boarding school located in Abuja, Nigeria. It was established in 2008 and is known for its serene environment, conducive for learning. The school offers a blend of Nigerian and British curricula, providing a well-rounded education to its students.

01

CORE VALUES

Hibiscus Royal Academy is synonymous with academic excellence. We offer a challenging curriculum, experienced teachers, and state-of-the-art facilities to ensure our students receive a world-class education.

02

Our Mission

To provide a rigorous and stimulating learning environment that empowers students to achieve academic excellence and reach their full potential.

03

Our vission

To be the school of choice for discerning parents in Abuja, recognized for its consistently high academic standards and its graduates' success in top universities worldwide
